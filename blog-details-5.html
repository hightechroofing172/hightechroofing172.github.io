Emergency Roof Repair Tips for Cornwall and Devon Homes

  • Patrick Moore
  • Sept 22, 2023

Unpredictable weather in Cornwall and Devon can lead to roof emergencies. Our post is your guide to tackle them. From quick fixes to long-term solutions, we'll help you keep your home safe. Be it a sudden leak, storm damage, or unexpected issues, our expert tips empower you to handle roof emergencies with confidence, preserving your haven amidst adversity.

When the Unexpected Strikes

Picture this: A torrential downpour accompanied by fierce winds hits your Cornwall or Devon home. Suddenly, you notice water dripping from the ceiling or hear the ominous sound of a damaged roof. In such moments, knowing how to react can make all the difference.


Immediate Actions

Safety First: Ensure your family's safety by moving to a dry area within your home. Watch out for water pooling on the floor.

Contain the Leak: Place buckets or containers under the leaking area to minimize damage to your furnishings.

Turn Off Electricity: If water is leaking near electrical fixtures or wiring, turn off the electricity supply to that area to avoid potential hazards.

Call a Professional: Contact a local roofing expert, like us, immediately. Explain the situation and schedule an emergency repair visit.


Temporary Fixess

While waiting for professional help, consider these temporary measures to mitigate further damage:

Tarp Covering: If safe to do so, place a tarp over the damaged area to prevent more water from entering your home.

Seal Small Leaks: Use roofing sealant or a waterproof patch kit to seal minor leaks temporarily.


Long-Term Solutions

Once the immediate crisis is under control, it's crucial to address the underlying issue:

Professional Inspection: Schedule a comprehensive roof inspection to identify the extent of the damage.

Insurance Claim: If applicable, contact your homeowner's insurance provider to report the damage and start the claim process.

Repairs or Replacement: Based on the inspection results, your roofing expert will recommend either repairs or a roof replacement.

Preventative Maintenance: After the repairs, consider regular roof maintenance to prevent future emergencies.


For more roofing tips and professional services, feel free to visit our website kmroofingltd.co.uk and get in touch with our expert team today!


Conclusion

While Cornwall and Devon's weather can be unpredictable, being prepared for roof emergencies can help you safeguard your home and loved ones. Remember, your safety comes first, so don't hesitate to call professionals for assistance. In times of crisis, rely on local experts like us to restore your roof and your peace of mind.

Stay safe and secure in your Cornwall and Devon haven, come rain or shine.
