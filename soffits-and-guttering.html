Enhance Your Home's Protection and Appearance

At LPA Roofing, we provide comprehensive fascia, soffit and guttering services, encompassing installation, replacement, and repair. Your home deserves the best protection against the elements while maintaining its aesthetic appeal, and our team is dedicated to delivering just that.

Our Services

Fascias

Fascias play a crucial role in protecting the roof's edge and supporting the lower edge of the bottom row of tiles. They also carry the guttering system and give your roofline a neat, finished look. We offer:

  • Installation of new fascias

  • Replacement of damaged or outdated fascias

  • Repair services

Soffits

Soffits are located beneath the fascia board and serve both aesthetic and functional purposes. They help ventilate your attic space, preventing moisture buildup and ensuring proper airflow. Our soffit services include:

  • Professional installation of new soffits

  • Replacement of old or damaged soffits

  • Repair services

Guttering

A well-maintained guttering system is essential for directing rainwater away from your home, preventing water damage to the roof, walls, and foundation. We provide comprehensive guttering services, including:

  • Installation of new guttering systems

  • Replacement of faulty or worn-out gutters

  • Repair of damaged gutters
