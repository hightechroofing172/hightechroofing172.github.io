About Us

We Are Leaders In The
Roofing Industry

At Roof On Texas, we take immense pride in being a Veteran-Owned roofing company with a rich history in the roofing industry. Established in 1996 and recently organized as an LLC, our roots run deep with a legacy of honorable service, reliability, and a commitment to excellence. With our experienced team of roofing professionals, we focus on delivering top-quality roofing services, seamless gutter installations, and storm mitigation solutions. As a Disabled Veteran-Owned Company, we bring a unique sense of dedication and attention to detail to every project we undertake.

Our primary mission is to provide outstanding services to our valued clients, offering tailored roofing solutions that stand the test of time. We understand the importance of your home and property, which is why we treat every project with the utmost care and respect. Whether it's an insurance claim or a roofing repair, you can trust us to handle your project with integrity and transparency.

We stand firm against the common misconceptions surrounding roofing companies and work diligently to show that there are honest contractors in the industry. Our commitment to honesty and integrity is the foundation upon which we have built our reputation, and it drives us to go above and beyond to ensure your complete satisfaction.

At Roof On Texas, we understand that your roofing needs are unique, and that's why we offer personalized solutions that fit your budget and requirements. As a testament to our dedication, we provide reliable services and exceptional craftsmanship, leaving you with a roof that not only protects your home but also adds value to it.

When you choose us, you're choosing a team that genuinely cares about your home and your peace of mind. Let us be your partners in safeguarding your property and ensuring that your roofing and gutter systems are in top-notch condition.

Experience the difference that honesty, integrity, and expertise make. Contact Roof On Texas today for all your roofing needs.
