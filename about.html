About Us

Welcome to KM Roofing: Discover Our Story and Expertise.

Discover KM Building and Roofing Ltd, your trusted source for premier roofing solutions across Cornwall, Devon, and beyond. With over 30 years of expertise, we deliver professional, high-quality roofing services that exemplify our unwavering commitment to excellence.

Our dedicated team understands the significance of a secure, stylish roof. We ensure remarkable results and a seamless experience throughout the process. As trusted tradesmen rooted in the community, our portfolio highlights proficiency in slate, tile, flat, and lead roofing, along with chimney construction and guttering expertise.

Our dedication to the community is unwavering, backed by our involvement in local initiatives. As a family-run business, we understand the value of trust and transparency. This drives us to offer honest pricing, clear communication, and the highest ethical standards in all we do.

We pride ourselves on reliable service and a reputation built on successful projects. Enhance your property's aesthetics and insulation with our skillful installations of gutters, fascias, and soffits. Count on us for emergency roofing repairs to swiftly restore security and peace of mind.

Choose KM Roofing for unmatched reliability, excellence, and a legacy of proven results. Together, we'll shape a secure and stylish future for your property.
