Flat Roofing Systems

Flat roofing systems are prevalent on porches, garages, and flats, and we specialize in installing various types:

TPO Roofing:

Known as Thermoplastic Polyolefin, TPO roofing boasts widespread trade acceptance due to its longevity and easy installation. We bring expertise in implementing TPO roofing systems, ensuring performance and positive attributes.

Rubber Membrane (EPDM):

EPDM, or ethylene propylene diene monomer, is a genuine rubber material designed to resist sun damage. Resembling materials like inner tubes or pond liners, our team excels in installing EPDM roofing for durability.

Modified Bitumen:

A single-ply roofing solution, modified bitumen resembles ice and water shields but includes a mineral-based wear surface. While standing mid-range in terms of cost, it reflects heat, reducing energy bills.

Built-Up Roof:

A traditional hot-tar-and-gravel roof composed of multiple plies of water-resistant materials. Although more costly, it remains a viable option for certain roofing needs.
